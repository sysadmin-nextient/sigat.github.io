I. THE PROPERTY

Facility Overview

A purpose-built Class A tractor-trailer storage yard at the end of the North Holland cul-de-sac — secured, railroad-adjacent, and with direct access to I-85.

138 N Holland is a Class A Tractor Trailer Storage Yard strategically positioned at the end of the North Holland Drive cul-de-sac in the City of Jefferson, Jackson County, GA.

The site is bordered by active railroad tracks, an established industrial facility — providing a secure, purpose-built industrial parking and storage with direct access to major interstates I-85.
